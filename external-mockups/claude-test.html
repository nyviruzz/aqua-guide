Plain-Language Summary

What does this mean for you?

The tap water in Suffolk County meets all federal safety standards. Contaminant levels for lead, nitrates, and PFAS are well below EPA limits. No boil-water advisories are active. Your water is sourced primarily from the Magothy Aquifer and is treated with chloramine disinfection. It's safe for drinking, cooking, and bathing — including for infants and immunocompromised individuals.
