What This Means for You

Updated April 8, 2026

Your tap water in Brooklyn (11201) meets all federal safety standards and is safe to drink directly from the tap. Lead levels are well below the EPA action level of 15 ppb, and disinfectant levels are within the normal range.

The moderate hardness reading means you may notice mineral buildup on faucets or showerheads over time, but this does not pose a health concern. Overall, your water supply scores in the top 15% nationally for quality.

One thing to watch: Buildings built before 1986 may have lead solder in pipes. If your building is older, consider running cold water for 30 seconds before drinking after long periods of non-use.
