Actions for Your Home

Flush Your Pipes Each Morning

Run cold water for 30 seconds before your first use. This clears any metals that may have leached from pipes overnight, especially in older buildings.

Daily Habit

Replace Your Filter Cartridge

If you use a pitcher or faucet filter, check the replacement schedule. Most filters lose effectiveness after 40 gallons or 2 months of use.

Every 2 Months

Descale Appliances Monthly

With moderate hardness, mineral deposits can reduce the lifespan of kettles and coffee makers. A vinegar rinse once a month prevents buildup.

Monthly

Know Your Building's Pipes

Homes built before 1986 may have lead solder in plumbing. Request a free lead test kit from your water utility to confirm safety.

One-Time Check

Store Emergency Water Properly

Keep 1 gallon per person per day for at least 3 days. Use food-grade containers and replace stored water every 6 months.
