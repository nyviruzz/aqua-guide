Actions You Can Take

Simple steps to keep your household water at its best

Flush Taps After Absence

Run cold water for 30 seconds before drinking if your faucet hasn't been used in 6+ hours. This clears any metals that may leach from pipes overnight.

Easy

Replace Your Filter Cartridge

If you use a pitcher or faucet-mount filter, replace the cartridge on schedule. An expired filter can harbor bacteria and actually worsen water quality.

Check Quarterly

Read Your Annual CCR

Your water utility publishes a Consumer Confidence Report every July. It's the most detailed local source for contaminant levels and treatment methods.

Easy

Test for Lead If Pre-1986 Home

Homes built before 1986 may have lead solder or service lines. A $20 mail-in test kit can confirm whether your specific plumbing is contributing lead.

Recommended
