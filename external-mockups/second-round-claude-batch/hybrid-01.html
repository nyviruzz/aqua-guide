Recommended Actions

Steps for Your Household

Filter Your Tap

An NSF-certified pitcher or faucet filter removes residual chlorine taste and trace sediment.

Flush After Absence

Run cold water for 30 seconds before use if taps have been idle for 6+ hours to clear pipe sediment.

Test Annually

Order a certified home test kit once a year to catch changes your utility may not yet report.

Check Your Pipes

Homes built before 1986 may have lead solder joints. A quick inspection can rule out internal risk.

Set Up Alerts

Get notified instantly when a boil advisory, violation, or quality change affects your area.
